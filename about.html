About ML0X

Teaching machine learning the way it should be taught — from the math up.

Our Mission

ML0X was created out of a belief that the best way to learn machine learning is to build it from the ground up. In an ecosystem increasingly dominated by high-level APIs and pre-trained models, there is a growing gap between people who use ML tools and people who understand how those tools work. We exist to close that gap.

Every guide on ML0X starts with the mathematical foundation of the topic, derives the algorithm step by step, and then implements it in code using only fundamental libraries like NumPy. This approach takes longer than copying code from a framework tutorial, but it builds a depth of understanding that pays compounding dividends throughout your career. When you have built a neural network's forward pass, backward pass, and optimization loop by hand, no framework will ever feel like magic again.

We cover the full spectrum of foundational ML: linear models, tree-based methods, ensemble techniques, neural network architectures, dimensionality reduction, clustering, and the mathematical underpinnings that connect them all. Our content is designed for practitioners who want to go deep rather than wide, who prefer understanding to memorization, and who believe that the fundamentals of mathematics and computer science are the most durable skills in a rapidly evolving field.

ML0X is part of a network of specialized AI and machine learning resources. Our flagship resource, zovo.one, provides a comprehensive directory of AI tools and comparisons across the entire ecosystem. Together with our sister sites, we aim to cover every aspect of AI and ML education, tooling, and practice.
